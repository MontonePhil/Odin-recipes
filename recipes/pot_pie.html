Chicken Pot Pie

Home made chicken pot pie.

This recipe was one that my father found online and showed to me. While an easy recipe to look up, this one has fond memories for me specifically. This recipe will give you the means to make a delicous home made chicken pot pie to enjoy.

Ingrediants
  • 1 box (14.1 oz) pie crust
  • 1/3 cup butter or margarine
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 3/4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
Steps
  1. Heat oven to 425Â°
  2. Using saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add onion; cook 2 minutes, stir frequently, until tender. Stir in the flour, salt, and pepper until well blended. Gradually stir in broth and milk, cooking and stirring until bubbly and thick.
  3. Stir in chicken and mixed vegetables. Remove from heat. Spoon chicken mixture into crust-lined pan. Top with second crust; seal edge and flute. Cut slits in several places in top crust.
  4. Bake 30-40 minutes or until crust is golden brown. During last 15-20 minutes of backing, cover crust edge with strips of foil to prevent excessive browning. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
